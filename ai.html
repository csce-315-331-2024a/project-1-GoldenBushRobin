Introduction to the purpose and use of CTFs.

What are CTFs?

Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions are cybersecurity challenges designed to test participants' skills in various security domains, including but not limited to cryptography, reverse engineering, web exploitation, and binary exploitation.

Types of Challenges

CTFs consist of different challenges, such as:

  • Web Exploitation
  • Reverse Engineering
  • Binary Exploitation

Examples of Challenges

Here are examples of challenges you might encounter:

  • Web Exploitation: Find and exploit vulnerabilities in a web application.
  • Reverse Engineering: Analyze and understand the functionality of a given binary or piece of code.
  • Binary Exploitation: Exploit vulnerabilities in compiled binaries.
